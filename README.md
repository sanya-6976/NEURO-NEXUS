NeuroAssist AI

Intelligent Rehabilitation Platform for Children with Cerebral Palsy

---

Overview

NeuroAssist AI is an intelligent rehabilitation platform designed to support children with Cerebral Palsy through accessible, engaging, and home-based therapy experiences.

The platform combines Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Assistive Technologies, Progress Analytics, Gamification, and Interactive Learning to empower children, caregivers, and therapists through personalized rehabilitation journeys.

NeuroAssist AI aims to bridge the gap between traditional physiotherapy and affordable digital healthcare by providing an adaptive rehabilitation ecosystem that can be accessed anytime and anywhere.

---

Problem Statement

Children with Cerebral Palsy often require long-term physiotherapy, repetitive rehabilitation exercises, and continuous clinical supervision.

However, several challenges persist:

- Limited accessibility to rehabilitation centers
- High therapy costs
- Lack of personalized monitoring
- Reduced engagement among children
- Difficulty maintaining therapy consistency
- Limited remote supervision capabilities
- Manual progress assessment methods

NeuroAssist AI addresses these challenges by leveraging AI-driven rehabilitation tools, interactive experiences, and intelligent progress monitoring systems.

---

Vision Statement

Empowering accessible and intelligent rehabilitation through AI-driven therapy, personalized insights, and engaging digital experiences for children with Cerebral Palsy.
